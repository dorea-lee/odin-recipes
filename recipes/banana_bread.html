Banana Bread

Description

A banana bread recipe taken from Youtube, adjusted for my own preference, please support the original uploader.

Ingredients

  • 215g flour, all-purpose or otherwise
  • 6g baking soda
  • 6g salt
  • 3g ground cinnamon
  • 120g brown sugar, or as much as preferred
  • 113g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 eggs, preferably large
  • 3 (or 420g) of ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tsp (5ml) vanilla extract
  • 60g of almond flakes or walnuts, optional

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (177C).
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together.
  3. Add in the cinnamon, salt, vanilla extract and mashed bananas and mix well.
  4. Add in the eggs and mix well.
  5. Add in the dry ingredients, flour and baking soda and fold it into the mixture.
  6. Finally, add in any optional ingredients as needed.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes.
