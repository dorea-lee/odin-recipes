Banana Muffins

Description

A banana muffin recipe taken from Youtube, adjusted for my own preference, please support the original uploader.

Ingredients

  • 3 (330g) ripe bananas, mashed
  • 60ml milk, any kind
  • 55g of oil (for moisture) or melted unsalted butter (for flavour)
  • 100g sugar, any kind, white for 'airy' and brown for 'chewy'
  • 1 tablespoon (12ml) vanilla extract
  • 300g flour, any kind, I've used a mixture of almond and all-purpose at a ratio of 2:1
  • 12g baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Optional, 50g walnuts or 60g dark chocolate chips, or any other

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (180C)
  2. In a large bowl, add the bananas, milk, oil (or butter), sugar and vanilla and mix well.
  3. Add flour, baking powder and salt to the mixture and mix until it is fully incorporated.
  4. Add in the optionals.
  5. Bake for 20-22 mins
